Privacy Policy

AI Job Searcher

AI Job Searcher respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the extension handles user information.

Information We Process

The extension temporarily processes:

  • User-entered skills
  • Selected country for job search

How Information Is Used

This information is used only to:

  • Search for relevant job opportunities
  • Rank jobs using AI-based matching
  • Display job listings inside the extension

Data Storage

AI Job Searcher does not permanently store user-entered information. Search data is processed only during the current request.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

Security

All communication between the extension and backend service is performed over secure HTTPS connections.
